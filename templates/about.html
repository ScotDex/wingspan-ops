So, What Is This Thing?

Welcome to the Wingspan Data Hub (it needs a better name, suggestions in the feedback form!). Fully developed and reconstructed by Dexomus Viliana as how previous IT guys made stuff then ghosted everyone annoyed me. The site is still under active development, but I have managed to regain most of the features of the classic "Short Circuit" tool.

The original infrastructure was getting a bit old, getting slower and more fragile that Zeratule responding to DM's. The goal was simple: redevelop its functionality into something fast, clean, and less likely to break.

What Can It Actually Do?

Well it looks kinda pretty, but it can actually do stuff also.

  • Live Wormhole Connections: See a real-time list of all known wormhole connections, so you can stop face-checking that C2 with a 2 billion ISK Loki.
  • Agent Leaderboard: A little friendly competition never hurt anyone. This board tracks agent scan counts, giving you a valid reason to resent your corpmates.
  • Character Lookup: Spell the name right and you'll never have to google someone again.
  • Route Planner ("Short Circuit"): Find the quickest path from A to B through the tangled web of wormhole space. It's like a GPS for people who enjoy the constant threat of being vaporized by Sleepers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is the data up to date?

Mostly. The wormhole connection data from our agents is real-time. Data from EVE-Scout and ESI (like kills) is updated every few minutes. So, it's fresh enough that it won't get you killed. Probably.

How is this different from Tripwire?

Think of this as a high-level strategic map, while Tripwire is your detailed, in-the-moment operational tool. This hub gives you the "big picture" of the wormhole network, while Tripwire is for tracking the chain you're actively in. They work best together.

I found a bug / I have a great idea for a feature!

Excellent! Please use the "Submit Feedback" link in the sidebar. All suggestions and bug reports are welcome, especially the ones that make me question my life choices.
