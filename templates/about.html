So, What Is This Thing?

Welcome to the Wingspan Data Hub (it needs a better name, suggestions in the feedback form!). This is essentially what happens when I see something old and decrepit and want to do some good. The project is still under testing and development by Dexomus Viliana, but we have regained most of the features of the original Short Circuit tool.

The original infrastructure was getting a bit old, creaking under the weight of a decade of internet dust. The goal was simple: redevelop its functionality into something fast, clean, and less likely to break than a promise in Jita.

What Can It Actually Do?

It's not just a pretty face. Here’s the toolkit at your disposal:

Live Wormhole Connections

See a real-time list of all known wormhole connections, so you can stop face-checking that C2 with a 2 billion ISK Loki.

Agent Leaderboard

A little friendly competition never hurt anyone. This board tracks agent scan counts, giving you a valid reason to resent your corpmates.

Character Lookup

Spell the name right and you'll never have to google someone again.

Route Planner ("Short Circuit")

Find the quickest path from A to B through the tangled web of wormhole space. It's like a GPS for people who enjoy the constant threat of being vaporized by Sleepers.
